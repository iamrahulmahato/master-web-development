What is ZenFit?

ZenFit is a fitness app that helps you track your workouts, set goals, and stay motivated. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned athlete, ZenFit has the tools you need to take your fitness to the next level. With ZenFit, you can:

  • Track your progress
  • Set and set reminders for your workouts
  • Track calories burned, distance walked, and more
  • Connect with a community of like-minded individuals
  • Log and analyze your workouts
  • Access a variety of exercise and workout options
