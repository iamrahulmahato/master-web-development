How to Play

Player Mode

Use the arrow keys to navigate through the maze:

  • ↑ - Move Up
  • → - Move Right
  • ↓ - Move Down
  • ← - Move Left

Reach the green exit square to win the game!

Bot Mode

Click the "Play Bot" button to watch the AI solve the maze:

  • The bot will automatically find the shortest path to the exit
  • Watch and learn from the bot's efficient path-finding
  • Try to beat the bot's performance in your next attempt!

After completing a maze, you can challenge the bot to compare your performance.
