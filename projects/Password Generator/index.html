What makes a password strong?

Learn the characteristics of a strong and secure password to protect your online accounts.

Long

The longer a password, the more secure it is. A strong password should be at least 10 characters long.

Complex

Strong passwords use a combination of letters, numbers, cases, and symbols to form an unpredictable string.

Unique

A strong password should be unique to each account to reduce vulnerability in the event of a hack.
