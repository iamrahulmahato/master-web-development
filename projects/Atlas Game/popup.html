ATLAS GAME

(Indian Cities Edition)


15

Rules:

  1. The game starts with a randomly selected Indian city displayed on the screen.
  2. The player must enter an Indian city starting with the last letter of the current place.
  3. The same Indian city cannot be used more than once during the game.
  4. The player has 15 seconds to enter a valid Indian city. If the time runs out, the game ends.
  5. The game continues until the player can no longer provide a valid Indian city or the time runs out.
