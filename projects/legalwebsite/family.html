Family Law

Family law is a legal practice area that deals with issues related to families and domestic relationships. It covers a wide range of matters, including divorce, child custody and visitation, spousal support, adoption, and domestic violence.

Our dedicated family law attorneys understand the sensitive nature of family-related legal matters and are committed to providing compassionate and effective legal representation. Whether you are facing a divorce, seeking custody of your children, or pursuing an adoption, we are here to guide you through the legal process.

Family law cases can be emotionally challenging, and our goal is to help you find practical solutions that protect your interests and the well-being of your family members.

Hindu Marriage Act, 1955

The Hindu Marriage Act governs the marriage laws for Hindus, providing regulations for marriage ceremonies, registration, and divorce procedures.

Special Marriage Act, 1954

The Special Marriage Act allows people of different religions or those who do not wish to follow personal laws to marry and register their marriages.

Dissolution of Muslim Marriages Act, 1939

This law provides grounds for the dissolution of Muslim marriages and outlines the procedures for divorce.

Guardians and Wards Act, 1890

The Guardians and Wards Act deals with the appointment and powers of guardians for the custody and care of minor children.

Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005

This law aims to protect women from domestic violence and provides legal remedies such as protection orders and maintenance.
