Criminal Law

Criminal law is the body of law that relates to crime. It encompasses everything from petty theft to murder. It defines what is considered a crime and prescribes the punishment for unlawful conduct.

Indian Penal Code (IPC)

The IPC is the primary criminal code of India. It defines various criminal offenses and prescribes punishments for those offenses.

Criminal Procedure Code (CrPC)

The CrPC outlines the procedures for the investigation and trial of criminal cases in India. It ensures fair and just criminal proceedings.

Indian Evidence Act

The Indian Evidence Act governs the rules of evidence in Indian courts. It defines what evidence is admissible and how it should be presented.

Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act

This law regulates the production, sale, possession, and use of narcotic drugs and psychotropic substances in India.

Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act

The POCSO Act is designed to protect children from sexual offenses and provide for the speedy trial of such cases.
